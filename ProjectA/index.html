[ Harkonnens ]

Scientific Name:

[ Harkonnen Sapiens ]

Discovered by / at:

Paul Atreides and House Atreides, ~5600

What Happened:

When the Emperor of the Space Guild gave House Atreides the planet of Arrakis, House Harkkonen, the previous owners of the planned waged war on the Planet. As the Harkkonens arrive, their cosmic powers create power booms on Arrakis soil.

Planet:

The planet of Arrakis, a planet full of natural resources beneath its deserts and dunes.

Interstellar Commute of Harkkonens

Their Evolution has made them immune to the harsh conditions of outer atmospheric travel. Their cosmic dust scatters as their travel paths imprint on the skies

Harkkonen Characteristics:

  • Hotheaded.
  • Thousands of years in the cosmos gave them cospic powers.
  • Power Booms capable of moving mountains
  • ...

What we know so far about [ Harkkonens]? Besides their cosmic powers and hotheaded nature, nothing else is known about them! Their inner politics and the true extent of their powers are a mystery!. Guidelines for human interaction with Harkkonens Do not anger them at any cost!
