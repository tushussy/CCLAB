"Suspirium" is a hauntingly beautiful song by Thom Yorke, best known as the lead singer of the band Radiohead. The song was featured in the soundtrack for the 2018 horror film "Suspiria," directed by Luca Guadagnino. “Suspirium" serves as a thematic centerpiece for the film, capturing its eerie and mysterious atmosphere. The lyrics are introspective and poetic, reflecting on themes of loss, longing, and transformation. The song's title is derived from the Latin word "suspiria," meaning sighs or whispers. This word perfectly encapsulates the ethereal and haunting nature of the song. Yorke's lyrics speak of fleeting moments and the passage of time, capturing a sense of nostalgia and introspection.
